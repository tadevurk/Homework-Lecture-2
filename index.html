About Us

Haarlem Student Inc. is a company which is run by five students in Haarlem. This company was
founded in 2020 to sell computers and laptops, especially to IT students. During the pandemic many
students struggled with choosing the right computers for their classes and this became our motivation
to set up this company.

As we mentioned above, we are students, and we know the position that they are in and therefore
we sell affordable products. We are continuously researching new technologies and applying these
possibilities to our products.
